Welcome

Welcome to the Section of campus life and Student Development Programmes. The main aim of campus life is to create a living and learning environment which addresses the quality of life for students. Our mandate is to provide the housing services which are comfortable and enhance personal growth. The department also strives to provide social and educational programmes and services such as counselling and providing disciplinary policies to ensure peace, order and interaction


Our Accommodation capacity is only 1200 slots with the first priority given to 1.1 students followed by 1.2 students. The rest of the students apply online on a first come first serve basis if there are any remaining slots. The governing instrument for all students is the Student Handbook (pay particular attention to Ordinance.
